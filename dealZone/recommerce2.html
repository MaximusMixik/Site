Electronics

The Electronics at Recommerce School focuses on extending the lifecycle of electronic devices through sustainable practices. Students will delve into the intricacies of refurbishing, reselling, and recycling a variety of electronic products, including smartphones, laptops, and household gadgets. The curriculum covers diagnostic techniques, repair skills, and market strategies to maximize the value and usability of pre-owned electronics. By learning to transform electronic waste into valuable assets, students will not only develop technical proficiency but also contribute to reducing environmental impact and fostering a circular economy. Through hands-on projects and real-world applications, the Electronics category equips future entrepreneurs with the expertise to thrive in the rapidly evolving recommerce industry.
