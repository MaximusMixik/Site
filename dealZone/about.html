About Us

Welcome to Recommerce School, where sustainability meets education! Our mission is to empower students with the knowledge and skills to thrive in the rapidly growing field of recommerce. By focusing on the principles of recycling, refurbishing, and reselling, we provide hands-on learning experiences that promote environmental stewardship and economic innovation. Our dedicated team of educators and industry experts are passionate about transforming the way we think about consumption and waste. At Recommerce School, we believe in fostering a community that values creativity, sustainability, and the power of second chances. Join us and become a part of the future of green commerce!
