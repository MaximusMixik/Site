How to start?

  • Step 1

    Introductory Call

    To get started, we need just a short 30-minute introductory call. During this time, we gather your requirements, understand your challenges, and outline the key areas we can assist with.

  • Step 2

    Pricing Proposal Call

    After the call, we provide a tailored pricing proposal based on your needs. Our transparent pricing ensures that you know exactly what you’re getting and at what cost.

  • Step 3

    Agreement and Start

    Once the pricing is agreed upon, we quickly move to signing the necessary documents. In most cases, we can have everything in place and go live within two weeks, ensuring minimal downtime and a fast, efficient onboarding process.

Continuous Communication and Reporting

Our team remains in constant contact with you, providing regular reports to keep you informed of our progress. Additionally, you’ll have a dedicated point of contact, available at any time to update you on the current state of your customer support operations.
